The Wolfeblood Chronicles

Landon Wolfe leads an ordinary life, aware of supernatural creatures only through stories and myths. This changes dramatically when he is bitten by a werewolf, revealing the reality of the supernatural world. He soon meets Charlotte Riverarrow, and together they venture into the mystical forest near their school. Crossing through an enchanted archway, they find themselves in the magical realm of Chadronia. Here, they encounter Rosa Sea, a relative and the empress’s daughter, who helps them navigate this new world. As Landon grapples with his newfound identity and powers, he experiences a vision of an impending battle in Chadronia, involving his granduncle Argon. Determined to uncover the truth and protect their new home, Landon, Charlotte, and Rosa seek guidance from The Empress, Eliza. Their journey leads them to uncover their family’s legacy and their roles as guardians of Chadronia. As they prepare for the upcoming Andromedan Luminara celebration, Landon realizes the depth of his connection to Chadronia and the divine legacy of Andromeda Crystal. Their adventure is just beginning, with destiny calling them to protect the realm from looming threats.
